About

Mohamed Elshorbagy is one of the most dominant professional squash players in the world. Born in Egypt, he later represented England and is known for his incredible fitness, mental strength, and aggressive playing style.

Throughout his career, ElShorbagy has achieved the World No.1 ranking multiple times and has won numerous PSA World Tour titles. He is widely respected for his consistency, leadership, and dedication to the sport of squash.
